# KPI Definitions

## Total Sales
Sum of all sales transactions.

Formula:
Total Sales = 
SUM(FactSales[Sales])
---

## Total Profit
Sum of all profit transactions.

Formula:
Total Profit = 
SUM(FactSales[Profit])
---

## Total Orders
Distinct count of Order IDs.

Formula:
DISTINCTCOUNT(FactSales[Order_ID])

---

## Total Quantity Sold
Total quantity sold across all orders.

Formula:
Total Quantity Sold = 
SUM(FactSales[Quantity])
---

## Profit Margin %
Profit generated per sales dollar.

Formula:
Profit Margin % = 
DIVIDE([Total Profit], [Total Sales], 0)
---

## Average Order Value (AOV)
Average revenue per order.

Formula:
Average Order Value = 
DIVIDE([Total Sales], [TotalOrders], 0)
